Voices of New Americans Blog:
I'm a Legal Immigrant Who Voted for Hillary

I've been called a socialist for supporting economic fairness, effective climate change policies, and safety nets for the nation's most marginalized people. They lecture me endlessly about cultural preservation. I spent years going through the process of legally immigrating from Germany and becoming an American citizen, but even I am sick of Republicans' bigotry toward undocumented immigrants who come here often as a desperate, last resort hoping to make better lives for their families.

I have saved every penny I could to provide a good home and a good education for my family, but Republicans show no such fiscal restraint spending trillions on an unnecessary tax cut for billionaires instead of on policies we need. I work long hours every day, yet unlike nearly the entire rest of the world my workplace is not required to offer paid parental leave. While I was afforded an excellent education at virtually no cost in Germany, American students are saddled with absurd college debt often well into their 40s because of brutal Republican policies.

As a legal immigrant and proud progressive, I am glad that my voice finally counted with my vote for Hillary. Republicans' time in power is quickly coming to an end.
